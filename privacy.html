Privacy Policy

At Snazzy Paws Grooming, We are commited to protecting all personal information of our clients.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with our website or services:

  • Personal contact details (name, email address, phone number)
  • Booking information related to your dog and chosen services
  • Messages submitted through our contact forms

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide you with grooming services. This includes:

  • Processing bookings and inquiries
  • Providing grooming updates or confirmations
  • Responding to questions or customer support requests

3. Data Protection

We take appropriate measures to secure your information and prevent unauthorized access. Only authorized staff members may access booking or contact information.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal details with outside parties unless required for essential service operation or by law.

5. Cookies & Website Tracking

Our website may use basic cookies for functionality or analytics. You can disable cookies through your browser settings if desired.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information at any time.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. All changes will be posted on this page.
